About Me

Hello, I'm Alex Johnson

I'm a front-end developer and UI/UX designer with over 8 years of experience creating digital products that users love. My passion lies in building interfaces that are not only visually appealing but also intuitive and accessible.

I started my career as a graphic designer but quickly transitioned to web development when I discovered the power of combining design thinking with technical implementation. This blog is my space to share what I've learned along the way.
